How Does It Work?

  • Shoppers submit "Requests" on Requiren.com, which lists the requirements of what they need to buy

  • We take that Request and pass it along to our top-knotch vendors that are capable of providing the Request

  • Vendors take a look and may make an offer to the Request

  • Once the deadline period has passed, the Requester can view & compare all offers to make their preferred purchase
